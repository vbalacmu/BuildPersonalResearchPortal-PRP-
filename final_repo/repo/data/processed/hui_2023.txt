JIAO YANG, ERCAI CHEN, RUI YANG* AND XIAOYI YANG
Abstract. This paper aims to investigate the thermodynamic
formalism of weighted amenable topological pressure for factor
maps of amenable group actions. Following the approach of Tsukamo to
[Ergodic Theory Dynam. Syst. 43(2023), 1004-1034.], we introduce
the notion of weighted amenable topological pressure for factor
maps of amenable group actions, and establish a variational prin-
ciple for it. As the application of variational principle, we show
weighted amenable measure-theoretic entropy can be determined
by weighted amenable topological pressure. Equilibrium states of
weighted topological pressure are also involved.
